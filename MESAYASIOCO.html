Welcome to the world of Information and Communications Technology

Information and Communications Technology (ICT) refers to the study and use of technologies that manage communication and information.It includes telecommunications,computers,software,storage devices,and audiovisul system that enable people to access,store,send and process information

Main ICT Topics

Computer Hardware

Hardware refers to the physical parts of a computer that can be seen,and touched.Examples includes the monitor,keyboard,motherboard,CPU, and the mouse

Software

Software consist of program and applications that run a computer.These include operating system,word processors,and web browser that allow users to perform different task.

Networking

Networking is the process of connecting computers and other devices so they can communicate and share data through the internet or local networks.

Web Development

Web development is the process of designing and building websites and web applications using programming languages such as HTML,CSS,and Javascript.

The Importance of ICT

ICT plays an important role in education,business,communication,and internet.It helps people share information quickly,improve productivity,and stay connected with others around the world
